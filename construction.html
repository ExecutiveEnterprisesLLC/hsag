HSAG provides restoration/remediation/reconstruction facilitation services to property owners to resolve damage resulting from expansive soils, water infiltration, frozen pipes, chemical spill, wind, hail and construction defects. If a chemical spill leads to a hazmat situation, we can quickly engage environmental specialists to properly assess affected areas and organize abatement services for safety, containment and restoration. If water damage leads to mold growth, environmental specialists can properly assess affected areas, abate mold per state and federal regulations, and cure any air quality issues.

HSAG understands that time is of the essence in these situations so we are prepared to mobilize on a moment’s notice. Once on site, we work diligently to facilitate the completion of all expert contract work in a safe, efficient, and quality manner.
